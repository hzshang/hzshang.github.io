About

Posted on Wed, Oct 20, 2021

Hi, welcome to my blog. I hope you can get some fresh ideas from here.

I'm a software and security engineer with a focus on binary reverse and exploits. I find bugs and manipulate the bytecode on operation systems, virtualization platforms, IoT firmwares, and anything hardcore.

In 2019, I graduated from Fudan University with a bachelor's degree in computer science. In college, I was the captain of the team ******. With my teammates, I played CTF games, and went to my first DEFCON CTF in 2018. After I graduated, I worked in Tencent Keen Security Lab as a security engineer for two years. I developed some fuzz gadgets for bug hunting in Tencent, and won the first DEFCON CTF champion in 2020 as a member of A*0*E. Currently I live in Shanghai, as a bug hunter all the time.
